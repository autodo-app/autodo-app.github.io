Own Your Car's Data

Every time you log a refueling of your car your repeating tasks get more accurate notifications.

Statistics on your car and its maintenance gives you the information you need to know how your car is performing, and the ability to easily create recurring tasks means you don't have to worry about forgetting a task again.

auToDo adds a set of common routine maintenance tasks by default, all of which recur on a mileage interval that you control. You can add add and remove tasks from this set of defaults to match the exact needs of your vehicle.

In addition to the Statistics tab, you can view a log of your recorded refuelings along with statistics about each particular entry.

Creating a standalone ToDo item is a breeze. Just enter the name and due date (or mileage) of the action and associate it with any or all of your vehicles.
